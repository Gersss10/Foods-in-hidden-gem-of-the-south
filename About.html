ABOUT

This website is an informative website about the delicious food restaurants at Hidden Gem of the South (Alfonso, Cavite). This website provides important informations about some restaurants that is located in Alfonso, Cavite such as Zenzen Japanese Cafe, Surugin Ramen House, Queens Strawberry Farm, Burgers Ahuy, and Crosswings. The aim of this website is to promote the city's businesses to catch tourist' attention. As we feature these really great food restaurants that serves a very delicious menu together with good staff services, we believe that they deserve more hype and publication. The authors aim to share this amazing food restaurants on people who were planning on a vacation here in the South Luzon. We hope that are website is enough way to help you in planning your vacation! About us: We are students of Polytechnic University of the Philippines Alfonso-Campus who are currently studying Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering, decided to make this website as a Final requirement in Computer Fundamental and Programing course. We, the creator of this website, both a resident of Alfonso, Cavite, created this website with full will of wanting to promote the city.
