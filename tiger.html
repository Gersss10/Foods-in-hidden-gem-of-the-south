If you like a cafe that has a close to nature vibe, this is a must visit for you!

The Tiger Alley Farm & Garden Café is a hidden farm & cafe in Alfonso, Cavite. It’s like a campsite but a cafe. They have buffet din and bonfire. Sadly, it rained when we went there. Nakakarelax dito! Perfect pag gusto mo mag unwind. 📍 The Tiger Alley Farm & Cafe, Brgy. Esperanza Ilaya, Alfonso, Cavite Must try: ✨ Nachos ✨ Tiger Special Burger ✨ Hawaiian Pizza ✨ Pasta ✨ Tsokolate de Batirol The Tiger Alley started serving burgers in Laguna. Eventually, they were able to bring it here in Alfonso. You can really distinguish that they make their own very delicious patties. Some of the ingredients they use like lettuce are also fresh from their farm. There's variety of dish to choose from the menu. Breakfast meals, burgers, pizza, pasta, and group bundles. They also have food buffet with Filipino cuisuine. Perfect for family bonding. What can you get: ☕️ Coffee and non-coffee drinks 🍽️ Filipino cuisine.
